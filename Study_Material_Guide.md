#📘 How to Access Your Study Materials

## 🎯 Easy Access Instructions

### **Step 1: Open Your Tracker App**
Navigate to your tracker folder and double-click `start-tracker.bat` or run `start_server.py`

### **Step 2: Access the Study Material Library**
In the tracker app, look for the new **"📖 Study Material"** tab in the left sidebar

### **Step 3: Explore the Four Views**

#### **📁 Browse All** (Default View)
- **Quick Access Cards**: Fast links to getting started guides
- **Top Tags**: Direct access to your 7 core tag libraries
- **Category Grid**: All materials organized by type
- **Study Guides**: Your lesson files

#### **🕒 Recent** 
- See what you've read recently
- Quick access to frequently visited materials
- Continues your learning where you left off

#### **⭐ Bookmarks**
- Star your favorite guides
- Easy access to bookmarked files
- Personalized learning library

#### **🔍 Search**
- Find anything by keywords
- Search titles, content, or topics
- Perfect for finding specific concepts
